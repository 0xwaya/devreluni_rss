### KPI

**KPI** (pronounced /keɪ piː aɪ/): Stands for Key Performance Indicator. It refers to a measurable value used to evaluate the success and effectiveness of DevRel initiatives and activities in achieving specific objectives and goals within the developer relations field. 
<br/>
KPIs provide insights into different metrics, such as developer adoption, engagement, satisfaction, content reach, pipeline contribution, community growth, product feedback, brand awareness, and contribution to revenue.
<br/>
 By tracking KPIs, DevRel teams can assess their impact, identify areas for improvement, and align their efforts with broader business objectives.
