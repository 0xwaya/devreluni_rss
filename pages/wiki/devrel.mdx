### DevRel

**DevRel** (pronounced /ˈdɛv ˈrɛl/): is short for Developer Relations. It encompasses the field of work or a team within an organization that focuses on building relationships with developers and nurturing the developer community.

 DevRel professionals act as a bridge between the developers and the company, advocating for developers' needs and interests while also promoting the company's technologies, products, and services. 

 They engage in activities such as providing technical resources, organizing events and workshops, offering support and documentation, gathering developer feedback, and fostering a vibrant and inclusive developer ecosystem.

The goal of DevRel is to establish strong connections, trust, and collaboration between developers and the organization, ultimately driving developer adoption, satisfaction, and loyalty.
